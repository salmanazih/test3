Our Menu

Chicken

Chicken Wings: Crispy and flavorful chicken wings tossed in various sauces such as buffalo, barbecue, honey mustard, or garlic parmesan. Chicken Satay: Skewers of grilled or skewered chicken served with a delicious peanut or soy-based dipping sauce. Chicken Tenders: Breaded and fried chicken tenders served with dipping sauces like ranch, honey mustard, or ketchup.

Beef

Beef Carpaccio: Thinly sliced raw beef served with arugula, parmesan cheese, and a drizzle of olive oil. Beef Satay Skewers: Grilled beef skewers served with a flavorful dipping sauce. Stuffed Mushrooms with Beef: Mushrooms filled with a savory mixture of ground beef, herbs, and cheese.

Sushi

Maguro (Tuna) Nigiri: Slices of fresh tuna atop small beds of vinegared rice, often garnished with a touch of wasabi. Sake (Salmon) Nigiri: Salmon slices placed on rice, sometimes with a dab of wasabi, offering a rich and buttery flavor. Ebi (Shrimp) Nigiri: Cooked or raw shrimp on rice, often secured with a thin strip of seaweed.
